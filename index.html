Why hats from Katalin Merksz Millinery London?

At Katalin Merksz Millinery London, we consider each hat as a piece of art!

Katalin (our milliner) draws her inspiration from combining different hat styles, face cuts and hair styles. She hand picks each accessory and trimming.

Among her celebrity clientele, Katalin has been the personal hat stylist for the recent Hungarian X factor finalist, Adel Csobot. Her hats are also worn by the famous Bollywood star, Neha Dhupia and the Indian Sufi and Jazz singer, Sonam Kalra.

Each hat is cut, steamed and perfected by Katalin herself!

Our hats

Every hat in our collection, is therefore, bespoke with the added advantage of customizing hats based on your specific needs, may it be with a certain dress, occasion, colour, shape or accessory in mind.

As women, we understand the importance of first impressions may it be for a business deal, meeting, a fine date or for that matter, covering our hair with a chic accessory when faced with “bad hair” days!

Having struggled to find that hair accessory that transcends beyond the typical definition of a hat that could pair up with day to day professional, chic and casual wear, we decided to come up with our own style of hats — hats that you would love to wear knowing that no one else would be seen wearing your hat!
